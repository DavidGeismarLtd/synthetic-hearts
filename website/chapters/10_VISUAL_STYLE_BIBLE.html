COLOR SYMBOLISM

Blue + Green = Purple:

When Romeo and Julienne are together, their colors mix to create purple light. This is the visual representation of their love‚Äîsynthesis, unity, transcendence.

Desaturation = Death:

When characters die, their colors fade to grayscale. Romeo's blue fades to gray. Julienne's green fades to gray. Only together in death do they create a faint purple glow.

Color Bleeding:

As the story progresses, colors begin to bleed across faction lines:

  • Green plants appear in the Machinarium
  • Blue technology appears in the Verdant Depths
  • Purple becomes more common
  • Visual representation of change

CONTRAST

Everything is designed in contrasts:

  • Blue vs. Green
  • Metal vs. Organic
  • Geometric vs. Curved
  • Cold vs. Warm

SYNTHESIS

The resolution is always synthesis:

  • Purple (blue + green)
  • Hybrid (metal + organic)
  • Balance (geometric + curved)
  • Unity (cold + warm)
  • Love (logic + emotion)

BEAUTY IN DIVISION

Both aesthetics are beautiful:

  • Mechanite: cold, precise, awe-inspiring
  • Genovore: warm, chaotic, breathtaking
  • Neither is "better"‚Äîthey're different

HOPE IN SYNTHESIS

The Hybrid aesthetic is the most beautiful:

  • Combines the best of both
  • Shows what could be
  • Represents the future
