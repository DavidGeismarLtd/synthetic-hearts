### **2151: The Spark**

**January 15, 2151 - The Verdant Massacre:**
A Mechanite extremist group called the "Steel Prophets" releases a genetic plague in the Verdant Depths. The virus targets Genovore genetic markers, causing rapid cellular degeneration.

**Casualties:** 127,000 dead in 72 hours

**Elara Vyr V's Response:**
"They have declared war on life itself. We will respond with the fury of evolution."

**The Genovore Retaliation:**
Within a week, Genovore bio-hackers unleash the "Rust Plague"‚Äîa nano-organic virus that corrodes cybernetic implants. Thousands of Mechanites suffer catastrophic system failures.

**Marcus Helion VI's Declaration:**

### **2151-2155: Total War**

The war consumed Neo-Helion. The vertical city became a battlefield, with each level contested.

**Major Battles:**

**The Siege of Machinarium (2152):**
- Genovore bio-titans (genetically enhanced giants) assault the upper city
- Mechanite defense drones and powered armor units hold the line
- 3-month siege ends in stalemate
- Casualties: 45,000

**The Flooding of Verdant Depths (2153):**
- Mechanites sabotage the bio-filtration systems
- Toxic waste floods the lower levels
- Genovores forced to evacuate
- Casualties: 89,000
- Environmental damage: catastrophic

**The Battle of Vestige Ring (2154):**
- Neutral zone becomes a war zone
- Unmodified civilians caught in crossfire
- Both sides commit atrocities
- Casualties: 156,000 (mostly civilians)
- This battle turns public opinion against the war

**The Quantum Bombing (2155):**
- Genovore extremists detonate an EMP weapon in the Machinarium
- Thousands of uploaded consciousnesses destroyed permanently
- Mechanites call it "digital genocide"
- Marcus Helion VI's brother among the dead
- Casualties: 67,000 (including 12,000 permanent consciousness deaths)

### **2156-2160: The Grinding Stalemate**

Neither side could achieve victory. The city was dying.

**The Cost:**
- Total deaths: 1.2 million
- Infrastructure damage: 60% of the city
- Economic collapse: 90% unemployment
- Famine and disease rampant
- Population exodus: 8 million flee to the Vestige Lands

**The Hybrid Emergence:**
In the chaos, some began to question the binary choice. Secret communities of "Hybrids"‚Äîthose who combined both technologies‚Äîformed in the ruins.
